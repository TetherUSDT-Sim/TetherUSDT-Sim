Welcome to TetherUSDT-Simulated

TetherUSDT-Simulated (USDTs) is a blockchain-based research simulation project that mirrors the core structure of fiat-backed stablecoins like Tether (USDT). Built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), this project offers an educational and analytical environment to explore the mechanics behind token minting, supply control, price stability, and smart contract architecture.
