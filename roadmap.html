Project Roadmap

Q1 2025 - Project Launch

Initial smart contract deployment on Binance Smart Chain, creation of USDTs token for simulation purposes.

Q2 2025 - Website & Whitepaper

Launch of official website, GitHub repository, and release of full academic whitepaper.

Q3 2025 - Community & Testing

Start of public academic testing, feedback collection from students and researchers, improvements to codebase.

Q4 2025 - Educational Use & Expansion

Integration into university labs and blockchain curriculums. Support for new networks and wallet integrations.

2026 - Open Contributions & Case Studies

Allow community contributions to simulations, collect use cases for future academic publications.
