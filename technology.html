Technology Overview

TetherUSDT-Simulated (USDTs) is implemented using the BEP-20 token standard on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). It is written in Solidity and strictly follows decentralized practices. The contract is immutable and includes no ownership, no upgrade, and no pause functions.

  • Written in Solidity (v0.8.x)
  • 20 billion fixed total supply
  • 6 decimal precision
  • Minted at deployment — no further minting possible
  • No central owner or admin privileges
  • Fully verified on BscScan
  • Compatible with Trust Wallet, MetaMask, and other BSC wallets
